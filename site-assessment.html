Our Methodology

We consider multiple factors:

  • Property Type: Commercial, multifamily, workplace, retail, or hospitality
  • User Demographics: Employees, residents, customers, or visitors
  • Parking Turnover: High turnover (retail) vs. low turnover (workplace)
  • Dwell Time: How long vehicles typically remain parked
  • EV Adoption Trends: Current and projected EV ownership in your area
  • Peak Demand: Maximum simultaneous charging needs
  • Growth Projections: 3-5 year expansion planning

Typical ratio: 1 charger per 10-20 parking spaces for most commercial properties, adjusted based on these factors.
