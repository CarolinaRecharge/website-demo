About Carolina Recharge

Local expertise, engineering precision, and a commitment to powering Carolina's electric future.

We Engineer Solutions. We Power Progress.

Carolina Recharge was founded with a simple observation: property owners across the Triad need EV charging infrastructure, but the existing options don't work for mid-market properties.

National providers focus on large institutional deployments. Local contractors install hardware but walk away. Neither provides the ongoing partnership that property managers actually need.

We built Carolina Recharge to fill that gap — combining electrical engineering expertise with a capital-efficient business model that makes EV charging accessible for apartments, offices, and commercial properties across Winston-Salem, Greensboro, and High Point.

This isn't guesswork. Every assessment is backed by precise electrical calculations. Every recommendation is grounded in data. Every installation is engineered for your specific property.
